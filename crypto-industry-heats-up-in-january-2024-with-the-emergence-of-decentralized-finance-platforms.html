Rise of DeFi Lending and Borrowing Protocols One of the most prominent developments within the DeFi space was the surge of lending and borrowing protocols. Individuals now have the opportunity to lend their digital assets, earning attractive interest rates, while borrowers can access funds without relying on centralized institutions. Stablecoins Gain Popularity In January 2024, stablecoins continued to attract attention within the DeFi landscape. These cryptocurrencies, pegged to traditional fiat currencies or other stable assets, offer stability and serve as a bridge between traditional finance and emerging digital assets. Numerous projects launched their stablecoins, providing users with stability amidst the crypto market's notorious volatility. 4.

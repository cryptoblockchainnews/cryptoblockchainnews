Section 1 - Understanding DeFi (around 100 words): DeFi, short for decentralized finance, represents blockchain-powered financial platforms and applications that enable users to bypass intermediaries such as banks. Offering various financial services like lending, borrowing, staking, and decentralized exchanges, DeFi platforms operate on smart contracts. By leveraging cryptocurrency as collateral and automating processes, DeFi eliminates the need for traditional, centralized institutions. This transparency and efficiency offered by DeFi platforms make them highly attractive and revolutionize how individuals interact with finance.

Enhanced Security and Privacy Measures With traditional identity verification methods relying on centralized databases, the risk of data breaches and unauthorized access remains a constant concern. Decentralized identity platforms take a different approach. By using blockchain's inherent security and cryptography, these platforms ensure that personal data is encrypted and distributed across the network. They allow individuals to share only the necessary attributes of their identity, rather than exposing their entire profile. This approach not only safeguards sensitive information but also addresses the potential misuse of personal data by organizations.

The Bitcoin Halving Event: The Bitcoin Halving event is a pre-coded mechanism designed to maintain the scarcity and integrity of Bitcoin, the world's first and most recognized cryptocurrency. Expected to occur approximately every four years, this event halves the rate at which new Bitcoins are created. With the next one set to occur in 2024, this event has sparked immense interest and speculation among investors and experts alike. 2.

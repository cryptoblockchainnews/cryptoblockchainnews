In 2024, DEXs have emerged as a powerful force driving the DeFi movement, attracting substantial attention from crypto enthusiasts and investors worldwide. The surge in popularity of decentralized exchanges can be attributed to their distinctive features, including enhanced privacy, improved security, and the ability to list a broad range of tokens that might not be available on centralized counterparts.
